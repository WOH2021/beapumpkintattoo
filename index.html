Japanese Dragons (Ryu)

Dragons in anime represent wisdom, strength, and protection. Unlike Western dragons, Japanese dragons are benevolent water deities associated with the seas, clouds, and heavens. Popular in anime like Dragon Ball, Spirited Away, and One Piece.

Meet The Artist

Hello, I'm BeaPumpkin

A passionate tattoo artist and lifelong anime enthusiast, dedicated to bringing the vibrant world of anime to life through ink.

My journey began at age 12, sketching my favorite characters from Sailor Moon and Dragon Ball Z in the margins of my notebooks. That childhood passion evolved into a career combining two of my greatest loves: tattoo artistry and anime culture.

After training under master tattoo artists in both Japan and the United States, I've developed a unique style that honors traditional Japanese tattooing techniques while embracing the dynamic energy of modern anime.
